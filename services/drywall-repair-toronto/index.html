Drywall repair FAQs

Can you match existing texture or orange peel?

Yes. We replicate light orange peel, knockdown, or smooth finishes with texture hoppers and hand trowels, then sand for a flush surface.

Do you repair drywall in condos downtown Toronto?

We work in condos daily. We confirm elevator bookings, protect hallways, and keep noise to a minimum for neighbours.

What sizes of holes can you fix?

We patch everything from nail pops to large 4x4 ft cut-outs. Anything larger becomes a board replacement, which we also handle.

How long before the wall can be painted?

With quick-set compounds we finish sanding and priming the same day. Painters can usually follow within 24 hours.
